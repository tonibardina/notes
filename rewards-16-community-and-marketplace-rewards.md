# 16. Community and Marketplace Rewards

## Core idea

SaaS ecosystems often rely on user contributions: templates, plugins, answers, integrations, reviews, workflows, datasets, or examples.

Rewarding contribution quantity can destroy quality.

## SaaS example: template marketplace

Bad reward:

- Points for each template published.

Likely result:

- Many low-quality templates.

Better reward:

- Installs
- Reuse rate
- Ratings
- Maintenance
- Business outcome
- Expert review
- Clear documentation

## SaaS example: community forum

Bad reward:

- Points for every answer.

Better reward:

- Accepted answers
- Durable helpfulness
- Updates when product changes
- Clear explanations
- Mentoring new users
- Expert verification

## Questions

1. What contribution creates real value?
2. What contribution is easiest to fake?
3. How do we reward maintenance?
4. How do new contributors gain visibility?
5. Should rewards be money, status, access, or influence?

## Activity

Design a contributor reward system for:

- Template marketplace
- Plugin ecosystem
- Community Q&A
- Integration marketplace
- Education community

Include:

- Contribution types
- Quality signals
- Recognition system
- Anti-spam rules
- Maintenance incentives
- New-contributor path
