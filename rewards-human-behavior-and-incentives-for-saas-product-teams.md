# Rewards, Human Behavior, and Incentives for SaaS/Product Teams

## Purpose

This is a practical learning guide for understanding rewards, human behavior, incentives, motivation, and product design. It is written for SaaS founders, product managers, designers, growth teams, customer-success leaders, sales leaders, and operators.

The central question:

> How do we design reward and incentive systems that help users succeed, strengthen product value, and avoid manipulation, metric gaming, or shallow engagement?

This guide includes:

- Core concepts
- Extensive summaries of key resources
- SaaS/product examples
- Real-life scenarios
- Discussion questions
- Activities and workshop exercises
- Design templates
- A capstone project
