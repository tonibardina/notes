# 17. Customer Success, Expansion, and Health Scores

## Core idea

Customer-success teams use incentives too: health scores, renewal risk, adoption targets, QBRs, and expansion goals.

These can either align teams around customer value or push premature upsells.

## SaaS scenario

A CSM is rewarded for expansion revenue. They push customers to upgrade before adoption is strong. The customer buys more seats but does not activate. Churn risk increases.

Better incentive system:

- Verified outcomes
- Adoption quality
- Stakeholder engagement
- Renewal confidence
- Support burden reduction
- Expansion readiness

## Health score design

A useful health score should include:

- Product usage signal
- Outcome signal
- Relationship signal
- Support signal
- Risk signal
- Expansion readiness signal
- Qualitative context

## Questions

1. Does health score predict renewal or just activity?
2. What important customer context is missing?
3. Could CSMs game the score?
4. Does expansion follow value?
5. Are CSM incentives aligned with customer success?
