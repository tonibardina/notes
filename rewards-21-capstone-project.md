# 21. Capstone Project

## Objective

Design a complete reward and incentive system for a SaaS product.

## Product options

Choose one:

- CRM for small sales teams
- AI analytics platform
- Developer observability tool
- Customer-support helpdesk
- HR performance platform
- Finance automation product
- Cybersecurity compliance platform
- Knowledge-management tool
- No-code automation platform
- Customer education platform

## Deliverables

### 1. Product context

Define:

- Target user
- Buyer
- Main job-to-be-done
- Core workflow
- Business model
- Activation moment
- Retention loop

### 2. User-facing reward system

Design rewards for:

- Onboarding
- Activation
- Habit formation
- Collaboration
- Mastery
- Referral or sharing
- Long-term retention

### 3. Incentive analysis

For each reward, explain:

- What behavior it encourages
- Why that behavior matters
- What metric tracks it
- How it could be gamed
- What guardrail prevents abuse
- How it supports autonomy, competence, or relatedness

### 4. Internal company incentives

Design aligned metrics for:

- Product team
- Sales team
- Customer-success team
- Support team
- Marketing or growth team

Make sure the teams are not rewarded for conflicting behavior.

### 5. Ethics and harm audit

Evaluate:

- User pressure
- Manipulation risk
- Fairness
- Accessibility
- Data privacy
- Overuse risk
- Customer trust

### 6. Final presentation questions

1. What user behavior are you trying to create?
2. Why is that behavior valuable to the user?
3. Why is it valuable to the business?
4. What could go wrong?
5. How would you know the system is working?
6. How would you know the system is causing harm?
7. What would you remove if users started gaming it?
