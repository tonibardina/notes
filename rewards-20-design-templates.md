## 20.5 Ethical reward checklist

1. Does this reward help users achieve their own goals?
2. Are we rewarding meaningful value or shallow activity?
3. Can users understand how the reward works?
4. Could users feel pressured, judged, or manipulated?
5. Could teams game the metric internally?
6. Could customers game the system externally?
7. Does the reward create unfair advantage for power users or large customers?
8. Are new users encouraged rather than discouraged?
9. Are guardrail metrics in place?
10. Would we be comfortable explaining this reward system publicly?
