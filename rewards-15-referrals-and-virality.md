# 15. Referrals and Virality

## Core idea

Referral rewards should optimize for high-quality customer acquisition, not raw invite volume.

## SaaS example

Bad referral program:

- "Get $100 for every demo booked."

Risk:

- Low-quality referrals
- Spam
- Brand damage
- Sales team waste

Better referral program:

- Reward when the referred company matches ICP, activates, and reaches a meaningful milestone.
- Reward both the referrer and the referred customer.
- Offer account credits, premium access, donation, or community status depending on context.

## Questions

1. Are we rewarding introductions or successful customers?
2. Is the incentive too transactional?
3. Could users spam referrals?
4. Does the referred customer also benefit?
5. What quality threshold matters?
